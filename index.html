What I learned about Visual Studios

To set it up you have to download the app and install Live Server. Then add a folder and go to visual studios. Finally go to Visual Studios and click on File> Open Folder and find the folder you made.


The console screen is a screen that shows you the code that you made. It is used to show what is happening in your code.

From your Visual Studios you have to right click on your file and press Open with Live-Server. Then you right click on your output page and click on inspect and then console.


To print something on the console screen you have to put console.log and type in whatever variable you want after it.
